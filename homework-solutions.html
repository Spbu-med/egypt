Problem 4 • Clinical [span_4](start_span)

Heating tumor tissue (50g, $c=3.5\,J/g^\circ C$) from $37^\circ C$ to $43^\circ C$.[span_4](end_span)

$\Delta T = 43 - 37 = 6^\circ C$

$q = m \cdot c \cdot \Delta T = 50 \cdot 3.5 \cdot 6$

$q = 1050\,J$

Problem 15 • Cell Biology [span_10](start_span)

ATP Hydrolysis Analysis: $ATP + H_2O = ADP + H_3PO_4$[span_10](end_span)

Temperature Formula: $\Delta G = \Delta H - T\Delta S$ Result
Standard (298 K) $\Delta G^\circ = -30.96\,kJ$ Spontaneous
Body (310 K) $\Delta S = 0.0345\,kJ/K \implies \Delta G = -20.67 - (310 \cdot 0.0345)$ -31.37 kJ
Cold (277 K) $\Delta G = -20.67 - (277 \cdot 0.0345)$ -30.23 kJ
